\section*{Exam rules}

The exam is divided in two parts:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{1st part} (midterm): written test (open book, can bring anything as long as it is on paper) on the topic covered in the first part of the course (until \textit{implementation of matrix algebra in photonics})
    \item \textbf{2nd part} you have two options:
        \begin{enumerate}
            \item oral test on the topics covered in the second part of the course;
            \item in-depth thematic analysis/project (can be carried out in small groups of max 3 students).
        \end{enumerate}
\end{itemize}
