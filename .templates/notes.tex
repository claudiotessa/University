LaTeX is excellent for typesetting mathematics. Here's an example of an equation:

	E = mc^2

	a^2 + b^2 = c^2 
\end{equation}

\section{Adding Figures}
You can include images using the \texttt{graphicx} package:
\begin{figure}[h!]
	\centering
	\includegraphics[width=0.5\textwidth]{example-image} % Replace with your image
	\caption{An example figure.}
	\label{fig:example}
\end{figure}
